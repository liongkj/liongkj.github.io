Research

My primary research interests lie in natural language processing (NLP), especially in its intersections with machine learning. Currently, my research are driven by two goals:

Reasoning: Building AI agents that with human-like reasoning capabilities to analyze and interplay with knowledge for real-world scenarios.

Representation: Finding a universal, interpretable and effective representation for knowledge inherent in natural languages, which will form a fundamental basis for downstream tasks including reasoning.
