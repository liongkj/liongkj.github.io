## About Me

I am currently an **AI/Data Engineer** at [Advanced Vision Analytics (AVA) Asia](https://www.ava.com.sg/), where I focus on developing and deploying machine learning systems and data engineering pipelines. I graduated with a Master's degree in Computer Science from [Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)](https://en.sjtu.edu.cn/) in March 2024, where I conducted research at the [BCMI Lab](https://bcmi.sjtu.edu.cn/) under the supervision of [Prof. Hai Zhao](https://bcmi.sjtu.edu.cn/~zhaohai/).

## Research Interests

My primary research interests lie in:

- **Machine Learning** - Model security and interpretability
- **Data Engineering** - Building scalable ML pipelines and systems

## Background

I am a Malaysian Chinese with a strong passion for technology. In my free time, I enjoy developing web applications and exploring new technologies. I completed my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering with First Class Honours from Asia Pacific University, Malaysia.
